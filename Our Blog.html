John Bett, Africa Regional Manager, embarked on
an inspiring journey to Tanzania's Mlola
Biodiversity Restoration (MBR) project. Amidst the
majestic Usambara Mountains, MBR champions
ecological restoration, biodiversity conservation,
and climate change mitigation.

At the Kwemfumbii site, John witnessed the
tremendous progress of planting over 8,000 trees.
He met the passionate MBR team, who work
tirelessly with local communities and authorities to
effect positive change. Challenges are met with
resilient solutions and unwavering determination.

Read more on their improvements on the Project
page.
